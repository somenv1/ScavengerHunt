## Step 3: Customize Your QR Codes (Optional)

If using **qrcode-monkey.com**:
1. Paste the Google Maps URL
2. Choose colors that match the theme:
   - Primary: `#e8728a` (pink)
   - Background: `#fff5f7` (light pink)
3. Download as PNG (300 DPI for printing)

## Step 4: Create Photo + QR Code Combination with AI

### Option 1: Use AI Image Generators
Use tools like:
- **DALL-E** (ChatGPT)
- **Midjourney**
- **Stable Diffusion**

**Prompt Example:**
```
Create a romantic photo frame that seamlessly blends a QR code into the design.
The photo should be of a couple [describe your photo], with a QR code artistically
integrated into the bottom right corner. Style: romantic, pink and rose tones,
soft lighting, elegant border.
```

### Option 2: Use Photo Editing Software
1. Open your photo in **Photoshop**, **GIMP**, or **Canva**
2. Place the QR code in the bottom right corner (100x100px at 300 DPI)
3. Add a soft border or frame around the QR code
4. Optionally: Lower the opacity of the QR code slightly and place it over a light area of the photo

### Option 3: Simple Overlay
1. Print the QR code separately on sticker paper
2. Attach it to the bottom right corner of your printed photo
3. Place the combined photo+QR in an envelope

---

## Step 5: Testing Your QR Codes

Before printing, test each QR code:
1. Scan with your phone's camera
2. Verify it opens Google Maps to the correct location
3. Make sure the location pin is accurate

---

## Printing Tips

- Print letters on high-quality paper (100gsm minimum)
- Use color printer for best results
- QR codes should be at least 2cm x 2cm for reliable scanning
- Test scan the printed QR code before sealing the envelope

---

## Quick Print Workflow

1. Open `letter-template.html` in browser
2. Edit the placeholder text with your personal messages
3. Print all 6 letters (Ctrl+P / Cmd+P)
4. Cut out your QR code + photo combinations
5. Glue them in the bottom right corner of each letter
6. Fold and seal in envelopes
